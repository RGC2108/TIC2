San Andrés is a beautiful island in the Caribbean Sea that is part of Colombia. It is known for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and coral reefs. The island is also home to a variety of marine life , making it a popular destination for snorkeling and diving.

The island is also home to a number of endemic species , including the San Andrés lizard and the San Andrés crab.

Things to do there:

  • Relax on the beach. San Andrés has many beautiful beaches, including Spratt Bight, which is known for its white sand and turquoise water.
  • Go snorkeling or diving. The island is surrounded by coral reefs that are home to a variety of fish and other marine life.
  • Visit the Johnny Cay Natural Park. This small island is a great place to go swimming, sunbathing, and picnicking.
  • Explore the island's culture. San Andrés has a rich history and culture that is influenced by its indigenous people, as well as by the Spanish and African cultures.
